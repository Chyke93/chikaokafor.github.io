Origin, Location

Makurdi, Nigeria is where I was born. It's a nice and quiet little city in the middle-belt region of the African country. I have always had love for two things: coding and design. In the beginning, I never actually thought about it professionally, but when I saw the opportunity to join a career path that would expose me to these two aspects, it was an easy decision for me.

Education, Profession

In 2016, I graduated from the University of Port Harcourt, Nigeria with a Bachelors degree in Animal and Environmental Biology. After my school years, I went back home to Makurdi to work as a sales manager at a bookstore for three years.
I took up coding in July, 2018 to finally kickstart my journey towards building a career in web development. In order to boost my professional portfolio.
I enrolled in the Break into Tech program at Skillcrush.com where I got hands-on training in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and Git.
